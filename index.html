Fish and Tank Care Tips

Make sure your aquarium is in an ideal location.

Water temp needs to be checked daily.

Try to replicate the natural environment of your fish.

Determine the correct amount to feed the fish. Don't overfeed.

Stock the tank with compatible species.

Clean the tank monthly and clear away alage on aquarium walls weekly.

Check water filter and pump monthly.
